My Process
1
Discovery & ICP Alignment
A short onboarding call to understand your target market, average deal size, and what a "great client" looks like for you.
2
Prospect Research & List Build
I use Apollo.io's full filter library to surface matching companies and contacts, then enrich the data in Clay.com for accuracy and personalisation signals.
3
Email Verification & Hygiene
Every contact is run through email verification to protect your sender reputation and maximise deliverability before a single email is sent.
4
Sequence Launch & Monitoring
A personalised multi-step outreach sequence goes live. Open rates, reply rates, and bounce rates are tracked and optimised weekly.
5
Reply Management & Handoff
Positive replies are handled and booked directly into your calendar. You receive a weekly report and full transparency at every stage.
